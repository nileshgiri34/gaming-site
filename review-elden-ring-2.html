Review: Elden Ring II

← Back to Home

Miyazaki’s next masterpiece expands the Lands Between with new regions, brutal bosses and deeper lore. The world feels more cohesive and alive, with dynamic events that change based on your actions.

Verdict

Score: 10/10

  • Stunning visuals and world design
  • Expanded combat mechanics
  • Challenging yet rewarding gameplay

If you loved the original, this sequel is everything you hoped for and more.
