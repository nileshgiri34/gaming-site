Review: Cyberpunk 2077 (2025 Edition)

a href="../index.html">← Back to Home

The long‑awaited 2025 edition of Cyberpunk 2077 promises to deliver the game CD Projekt Red always envisioned. Improved AI, overhauled perk trees and next‑gen visuals breathe new life into Night City.

The most notable change is the refined police response system that reacts more believably to your actions. Combat feels tighter thanks to new skill synergies and a dynamic perk progression. On PS6 and PC, the ray‑traced lighting and detailed textures make Night City look stunning.

Verdict

Score: 9/10

Pros

  • Immersive open world
  • Deep character customization
  • Significant AI improvements

Cons

  • Occasional performance hiccups
  • Story pacing still uneven at times

Overall, this update makes Cyberpunk 2077 feel like a fresh experience, even if you played the original. Fans of sci‑fi RPGs shouldn’t miss it.
